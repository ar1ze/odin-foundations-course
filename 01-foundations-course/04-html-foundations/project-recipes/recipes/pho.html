A steaming bowl of beef pho brims with tender slices of beef, rice noodles, and fragrant herbs floating in a rich, aromatic broth. Wisps of steam rise, carrying the scent of star anise, cinnamon, and fresh lime.

Ingredients

  • Beef bones
  • Rice noodles
  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Star anise
  • Fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Prepare the broth - Roasat beef bones, onion, and ginger, then simmer with water, star anise, cinnamon, and cloves for several hours
  2. Season the broth - Add fish sauce, salt, and rock sugar to balance flavor.
  3. Cook the noodles - Boil rice noodels until tender, then rinse in cold water.
  4. Slice the beef - Cut thin slices of brisket or sirloin for quick cooking in hot broth.
  5. Assemble and serve - Place noodles and beef ina bowl, pour hot broth over, and top with herbs, lime, and bean sprouts.
